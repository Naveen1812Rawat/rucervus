Live Streams

Livestreaming refers to online streaming media simultaneously recorded and broadcast in real-time. It is often referred to simply as streaming. Live streaming has now became a platform for many gamers to show up their skills and make money.
Like youtube we can live stream our gaming skills on many other platforms.

Live Streaming Platforms

  •   Beyond Social media Facebook is also a live streaming and content sharing platform where we can share the uniqueness in us by uploading the content or live streaming.
  •   Youtube is an ultimate live streaming platform which can support a huge number of viewers at a time.
  •   Twitch is a gamer only platform where gamers play live.
